How to buy

Learn how buy the token

1. CREATE A WALLET

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

 

2. GRAB SOME BNB

have BNB in your wallet to switch to $CAL. If you don’t have any BNB, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

 

3. GO TO PANCAKESWAP

Go to https://pancakeswap.finance/swap in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $CAL token address into pancakeswap, select Calcium, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

4. SWAP

Swap your BNB for $CAL Set the desired slippage, click “buy,” and be prepared to adjust the slippage during times of market volatility if necessary.
